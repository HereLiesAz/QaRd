## Project Overview

Share your contact info, files, or social media with a QR code widget. A simple yet customizable QaRd design to share information quickly without digging through apps.

## Features

*   **Instant Access:** Keep a QR code of your choice always visible on your screen. Perfect for sharing your Wi-Fi password, contact details, a link to your portfolio, or any other information you need to share frequently.
* **Gestures & Interaction:**
    * **Single Tap:** View the decoded content of your QaRd in a dedicated detail view.
    * **Double Tap:** Quickly open the configuration screen to edit your code.
* **In-App Creation & Export:** Create QR codes directly in the app and export them as PNG images to
  your gallery (`Pictures/QaRd`).
*   **Fully Customizable:** Make the widget your own!
    *   **QR Code Data:** Set any text you want to be encoded.
    * **Shape:** Choose between classic square, circle, rounded squares, or diamond designs.
    *   **Colors:** Pick custom foreground and background colors to perfectly match your wallpaper and theme.
* **Simple and Lightweight:** The app is easy to use. Just a focus on doing one thing well.
* **Multiple Widgets:** Add multiple QaRds to your screen, each with its own unique data and style.

**How To Work It:**

1. Add the QaRd widget to your home screen or open the app to create a new one.
2.  On the config screen, include your QaRd info.
3.  Customize the QaRd design.
4. Huzzah. Your home screen now displays your QaRd. Resize for the perfect fit—it stays crisp!

## Lock Screen Availability

**Important Note on Lock Screen Availability:**

The ability to place widgets directly on the **lock screen depends** on your Android version and
device manufacturer. It is officially supported on devices like the Pixel Tablet.

For all other supported Android devices, the widget works perfectly on the **home screen**.
